## Toast

A toast is used for confirmations, alerts, and acknowledgments that require minimal user interaction, often displayed using a snackbar group.
Toasts appear at the bottom right of the screen and overlay any content.

### Toast variants

The component has 4 variants such as "info", "success", "warning", "error".
